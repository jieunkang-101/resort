Resort Activities

The following activities are included with your stay:

  • Weekly yoga in the fitness center.
  • One yacht excursion for two people.
  • Water aerobics classes daily at each pool.

The following activities are coordinated by our staff and available for an extra fee:

  • Helicopter ride around the surrounding islands.
  • Guided hikes.
  • Wine tastings.
  • Chef tasting menus at each restaurant.
  • Snorkeling and scuba diving.
  • Fishing trip with guide.
